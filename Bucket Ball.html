BUCKET BALL

Unreal Engine 5 project

Bucket Ball is a game I developed using Unreal Engine 5, using the in-built blueprint system. I learnt many workflows during my time in development, such as animations, asset building and particle systems.

In the first stages of development, I white-box prototyped the setting that the player will play in. I used Unreal Engine 5's landscape tools combined with a custom landscape material that automatically changes texture based on landscape steepness. Through development I am also practicing revision control with GitHub.

Further into development, I used Adobe's Mixamo to find a character model and animations, then created my own blend spaces to handle these animations. I then focused on core gameplay mechanics of throwing basketballs into hoops, paired with a UI system to display the score and current level to the player.

In the last stages of development, I focused on asset building. My workflow was modelling and UV mapping using Maya, then painting textures with Substance Painter 3D. I used Photoshop to create the target pictures in the goal props.

Left click: rotate (hold to pause rotation), scroll wheel: zoom
