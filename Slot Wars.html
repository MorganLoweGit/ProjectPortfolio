SLOT WARS

Unreal Engine 5 project

During my first semester of my second year at university, I challenged myself to also develop a 3d slot car racing game, using Unreal Engine 5. I took this opportunity to understand spline components and how they can be used in the scene, from the race track, to guardrails, to procedural content generation.

During the first stages of development, I learnt how spline components worked and created a prototype of the track spline actor. Due to having previous experience with Bezier curves, I learnt quickly and began to understand all the methods that the spline component offers.

Next, I focused on programming the main car actor, and how it follows the spline, to simulate a slot car race. During this I updated a UI system alongside my work, which I felt accelerated my progress, as I never needed to backtrack.

In the later stages, I studied UE5's procedural content generation tools to create forests around the racetrack, and reusing my custom spline logic for barriers along the circuit. I also added an AI opponent actor with a variable difficulty slider.
